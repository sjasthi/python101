## Topics

- What is List Comprehension?
- Syntax: `[expression for item in iterable if condition]`
- Mapping — transforming every element
- Filtering — keeping elements that meet a condition
- Mapping + Filtering — both at once
- `if...else` in the expression vs. `if` as a filter
- Common mistakes (using `()` instead of `[]`)
